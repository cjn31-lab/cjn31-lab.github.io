About Me

I started doing theater when I signed up for the drama elective at my middle school in Cooper City, Florida. I stuck with it, but didn't really gain the passion for it until I was in high school doing a professional production of The Little Mermaid. No one told me the theater bugs bite was permanent.I am currently a senior BFA Musical Theater student at the University of Miami. After graduating, the plan is to move wherever the work takes me to start my life as a professional actor.
